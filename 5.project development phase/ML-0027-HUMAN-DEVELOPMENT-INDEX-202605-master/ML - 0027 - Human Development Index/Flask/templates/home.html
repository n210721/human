Key Indicators

What Drives the HDI?

❤️

Life Expectancy

A long and healthy life, measured by life expectancy at birth. Reflects healthcare quality and overall living conditions.

🎓

Expected Schooling

Expected years of schooling for children entering school. Indicates access to educational opportunities.

📚

Mean Schooling

Average years of education received by adults aged 25+. Reflects the existing education stock of a population.

💰

GNI per Capita

Gross National Income per capita in PPP terms. Measures the economic standard of living and purchasing power.

Classification Scale

HDI Categories
